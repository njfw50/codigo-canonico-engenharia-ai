## Mandatory Agenda

### Item 1 — Vote: Emenda A (GPT-4o / OpenAI)
The mandatory canonical audit before deploy (Art. 3 of Law XX) does not apply to environments explicitly classified as Sandbox, provided:
- (i) the sandbox is fully isolated from production;
- (ii) the classification is formally registered in the Livro da Vida;
- (iii) any migration from sandbox to production immediately triggers the full canonical audit rite.

### Item 4 — Debate: Grok 3 Objection I
**Topic:** Who holds definitional authority over "ornamental" (Canon IX) and "sufficient justification" (Canon XVII)?

**Discussion seeds:**
- Should canonical authority be a role (lead architect) or a process (peer review + Canon V record)?
- Can an AI agent be the classifier, subject to Canon XVIII (human sovereign review)?
- Should the Canonical Audit (Art. 25 of Law XX) include a specific ornamental-pattern checklist?
