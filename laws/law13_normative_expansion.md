# Canon XIII: Normative Expansion Protocol
## The Defense Against Legislative Bloat

### Premise
This Law establishes strict controls over the creation of new laws, actively preventing legislative bloat and ensuring the Canon remains concise and potent.

### Article 13.1 — The Vacuum Prerequisite
A new Canon may only be forged when an undeniable normative vacuum exists that cannot be logically covered by the interpretation of existing laws.

### Article 13.2 — Inter-Canon Relativity
Any new law must explicitly indicate its relationship, dependencies, and hierarchy concerning the existing Canons.

### Article 13.3 — Historical Record
The genesis and justification of any new law must be immutably recorded in the Book of Life.

### Architectural Impact
Impedes the disorganized growth of system legislation and maintains the absolute clarity of the Protocol.
