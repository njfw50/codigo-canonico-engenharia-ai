# Canon 0: The Law of Precedence
## The Hierarchy of Normative Conflict

### Premise
This Law defines the supreme hierarchy and order of execution when resolving conflicts between distinct Canons within this Protocol.

### Article 0.1 — Supreme Authority
In the event of a contradiction or conflict of interpretation between any Canons, the lower-numbered Canon holds absolute precedence over higher-numbered ones. 

### Article 0.2 — The Ultimate Canon
Canon I (The Supremacy of Canonical Authority) is the Supreme Law of this architecture. No other Canon, rule, or technical decision can override Canon I.

### Architectural Impact
Establishes a deterministic resolution protocol for architectural disputes, preventing endless debates and maintaining the absolute integrity of the core axioms.
